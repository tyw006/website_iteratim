About the Studio

Crafting AI-Powered
Mobile Experiences

Iteratim Studios is an independent mobile app studio that leverages AI tools and technologies to build intelligent, modern iOS and Android apps. Created and maintained by a solo developer passionate about the intersection of AI and mobile development.

The name "Iteratim" comes from the Latin word for "repeatedly" — reflecting our philosophy of continuous improvement, using AI to accelerate development while refining each app until it's just right.

Our Approach

  • ✦AI-enhanced development — leveraging cutting-edge AI tools to build faster and smarter
  • ✦Intelligent features — integrating AI/ML capabilities into apps
  • ✦Modern tech stack — Swift, Kotlin, React Native, and AI frameworks
  • ✦Open to collaboration — interested in AI-powered projects
