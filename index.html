The studio

The name is
a methodology.

Iteratim comes from the Latin for repeatedly — our conviction that great software is found, not written. Every feature ships, gets used, gets reshaped.

We build what people thought was out of reach, then keep compounding on it. AI is the leverage that makes that rhythm real — faster prototypes, tighter loops, smarter features inside the product itself.

01

AI as the engine, not the ad

Multimodal LLMs and vision disappear into features that feel intuitive, not clever.

02

Cross-platform by default

React Native and Flutter, so iOS and Android ship from one codebase — without looking like web in a wrapper.

03

Ship, learn, iterate

Small releases, real users, steady rewrites. The name is the methodology.

04

Privacy as a feature

No ads, no tracking, no selling data. Subscriptions that fund the work.
